The Work

Module One

Family Dynamics and the 2nd-Gen Handoff

By the end of module one, you have a working structure for how decisions get made - and by who - in the family operation.

The hardest part of running a family trade business is not the roofing. It is the authority dynamics between generations, the crew expectations when the son or daughter takes over, and the conversations most family operators avoid until a crisis forces them. This module gives you the structure before the crisis.

Module Two

Sales Process - How We Close Without Pressure

By the end of module two, you have a quoting conversation structure that holds margin without feeling like a pitch.

The specific conversation flow we use at Masters Roofing Memphis. How we handle the price objection. How we build options. How we quote jobs that are worth taking versus jobs that look good on paper and drain the crew. Includes the pushback script we use with homeowners word for word.

Module Three

Brand Building on a Trade Budget

By the end of module three, you have a 3-pillar content plan and a scorecard bio-link funnel live.

How we built @mastersroofing.memphis to 25K followers and landed brand partnerships without a marketing team. The specific content structure, the brand partnership approach, and the lead magnet setup that turns casual followers into email subscribers you can market to indefinitely.

Team Management for Family Trade Shops

By the end of module four, you have a crew retention structure and a weekly ops rhythm that runs without daily firefighting.

Hiring, crew retention, pay structures that keep good people past the 18-month mark, and the Friday debrief that sets up every following week. Built for small family trade shops where turnover hits hard and the people are relationships, not just employees.

FAQ

Common questions, before applying.

Do I need to be a 2nd-gen roofer to get value from this?

No. The course is built for anyone running or building a family roofing operation. The 2nd-gen handoff module is specific to that situation, but modules 2-6 (sales, brand, team, reputation, and national brand) apply to any family trade operator building a real roofing business.

Is this a Memphis-specific course?

No. The sales process, team management, brand building, and content strategy work in any market. Memphis is the context for the case study, not the product. Tampa, Dallas, Atlanta, Chicago - the systems install the same.

How long are the modules?

Each module is 20-40 minutes of video. Self-paced. No live calls required. You watch when it fits your schedule and run the install on your own timeline. Most operators get the first wins in module 2 (sales) within the first 7 days of enrolment.

What is the price?

The Masters Method is priced to be accessible for operators at any revenue stage. Apply to see the current enrolment price - it is a one-time payment with lifetime access. No subscription, no ongoing fees.

I have an established roofing company at $2M+ revenue. Is this still for me?

The brand-building and content modules (3 and 6) are relevant at any revenue stage. Modules 1-2 and 4-5 are built for operators still in the system-building phase - if you are past $3M with full processes, you will get more value from the brand and team modules than the foundational ones. The application will route you to the right track.

What if I do not get value?

Complete the first two modules. If it is not useful, full refund within 14 days of enrolment. No questions.
