FAQ

Common questions, before applying.

Do I need to be a 2nd-gen roofer to get value from this?

No. The mentorship is built for anyone running or building a family roofing operation. The 2nd-gen handoff pillar is specific to that situation, but pillars 2-6 (sales, brand, team, reputation, and national brand) apply to any family trade operator building a real roofing business.

Is this Memphis-specific?

No. The sales process, team management, brand building, and content strategy work in any market. Memphis is the context for the case study, not the product. Tampa, Dallas, Atlanta, Chicago - the systems adapt the same. Part of the mentorship is making that adaptation with you live.

How does the mentorship run?

Weekly live cohort calls with Andrew, 1:1 office hours, and async support between sessions. Small group - 30 to 60 operators maximum per cohort. You bring your specific situation. Most operators see the first real wins from pillar 2 (sales) within the first week they apply it.

What is the investment?

The Masters Method Mentorship is a small-group cohort at a price point that reflects direct access to Andrew - not a self-paced product. Apply to see the current cohort pricing and whether there is still a seat available. No pressure either way.

I have an established roofing company at $2M+ revenue. Is this still for me?

The brand-building and content pillars (3 and 6) are relevant at any revenue stage. Pillars 1-2 and 4-5 are built for operators still in the system-building phase - if you are past $3M with full processes, you will get more from the brand and team sessions than the foundational ones. The application conversation will route you to the right fit.

What if I do not get value?

Attend the first two cohort sessions. If it is not useful, full refund within 14 days of your start date. No questions.
